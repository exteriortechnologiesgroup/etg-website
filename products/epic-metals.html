EPIC Metals

Architectural Deck & Roof Systems Since 1968

For more than 45 years, EPIC Metals has been practising the Art and Science of structural deck engineering. Wideck, Super Wideck, Epicore, and Archdeck systems enable designers to create unique, visually open public spaces with exposed structural deck ceiling appearances.

EPIC's deck systems eliminate the conventional ceiling tile — the structural deck itself becomes the finished architectural surface. Long spans, integrated acoustic performance, and concealed mechanical capability define every product line.

// CASE STUDY — PAN AM AQUATICS

Exposed Deck Ceiling System

The Pan Am Aquatic Centre showcases EPIC Metals' approach to architectural deck — using the structural roof system itself as the visible ceiling. The continuous Wideck profile delivers acoustic performance, long spans across the pool deck, and a clean modern finish without conventional ceiling assemblies.

The same project pairs EPIC Metals deck above with ALPOLIC/fr panels at the central stairway — demonstrating cross-product coordination.
