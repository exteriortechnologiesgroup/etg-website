Curved & Complex Cold-Formed Framing

The premier provider of cold-formed steel framing solutions for curved and complex surfaces. Domes, curved ceilings, walls, façades, and other complex surfaces. Curved-Right custom framing, BIM/3D modeling, and unitized solutions out of Minneapolis since 1996.

Radius Track manufactures pre-curved cold-formed steel framing components from Revit and Rhino models — eliminating field-bending, reducing installer rework, and unlocking architectural geometries that would otherwise be cost-prohibitive.

// CASE STUDY — TTC YORK STATION

Free-Form Aluminium Roof

The 407 TTC Subway Station by AECOM & Aedas required a free-form aluminium roof — geometry that defied conventional sub-frame construction. Radius Track Curved-Right framing was modelled directly from the Aedas Rhino file and manufactured to project geometry.

Kalzip XT Standing Seam panels installed onto the Radius Track sub-structure to deliver the architectural intent. The combined system won the 2017 CanBIM GC Award.
