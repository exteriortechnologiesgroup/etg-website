System &
Performance

Favemanc terracotta is extruded with a honeycomb cross-section that delivers exceptional rigidity, thermal mass, and weight reduction compared with solid tile. Combined with the ventilated cavity behind the panels, the system delivers measurable energy efficiency and acoustic performance.

// CASE STUDY — VENTILATED FAÇADE

Energy Efficiency Through Geometry

Ventilated façade systems represent the leading approach to energy-efficient cladding in European markets. The open cavity behind the tile drives a passive chimney effect — reducing solar gain in summer and managing moisture year-round.

Favemanc's extruded honeycomb tile section reduces overall weight while maintaining the thermal mass benefit of ceramic. Combined with concealed clip fixings and a wide glaze palette, the system delivers premium aesthetics without the performance compromises of veneer alternatives.
