Capabilities

System &
Performance

ForMe Design products are 100% extruded aluminium — fully recyclable, dimensionally stable, and immune to the warping that affects roll-formed competitors. Combined with Powder on Powder woodgrain technology, the result is a premium material indistinguishable from real wood at distance.

// FEAT 01

SKIN Line

Architectural cladding planks in widths from 30mm to 200mm. Concealed-fastener install with ventilation gaps.

// FEAT 02

DOOR Line

Garage door, entrance door, and gate cladding with matching aesthetic to the SKIN cladding line.

// FEAT 03

Powder on Powder

Patented woodgrain finish — two layers of powder coating create authentic woodgrain depth and texture.

// FEAT 04

Sublimation

Heat-transfer woodgrain technology for full-depth pattern that does not chip, peel, or fade.

// FEAT 05

HD Printing

Direct-print technology for custom patterns including stone, concrete, and abstract finishes.

// FEAT 06

Battens

BATTENS line — narrow-width vertical & horizontal slats for façade rhythm and texture.

// CASE STUDY — ARCHITECTURAL SOFFITS

Beyond the Façade

ForMe Design soffits offer a blend of style and functionality that elevates the exterior beyond the façade. The SKIN cladding line wraps from wall to soffit in a continuous aluminium plane — eliminating the conventional break between vertical and horizontal surfaces.

The Powder on Powder finish delivers the warm visual character of wood with the durability, fire performance, and decade-long colour stability of architectural aluminium. The 15-year warranty is industry-leading.
