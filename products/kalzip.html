Kalzip

Standing Seam Aluminium — World's #1

Kalzip is the world-leading standing seam roofing and wall cladding system. Over 1.3 billion square feet have been installed globally. Innovative, long-lasting, high-performance building envelope solutions using aluminium profiled panels.

// CASE STUDY — SAIT CALGARY

750,000 sq ft of Standing Seam

The Southern Alberta Institute of Technology required an envelope solution that could deliver LEED accreditation, complex curved geometry, and Calgary's extreme thermal cycling — all within a single coordinated install.

Kalzip XT Standing Seam was specified across 750,000 sq ft of roof and wall surface. On-site roll-forming eliminated transport length limitations, and the E-Clip thermal break system delivered measurable energy performance improvement over conventional fastener-through systems.
