// PARKLEX PRODEMA CANADA · OVERVIEW

Parklex Prodema
by Exterior Technologies Group.

Exterior Technologies Group brings Parklex Prodema — the Spanish specialist in engineered natural timber — to architects and specifiers across Canada. Using WoodResist technology, real wood veneers are transformed into panels that resist fire, water, and wear, then installed on ventilated façades, suspended ceilings, and interior walls with no further maintenance.

What sets Parklex Prodema apart is the surface: a layer of genuine natural timber rather than a printed paper décor. Every panel carries the depth, grain, and tonal variation of real wood — the aesthetic naturalness that wood-imitation products cannot reproduce — while the engineered core does the technical work behind it.

That core is what makes the timber behave. The boards are built to shrug off the most demanding exposure: after 3,000 hours of accelerated weathering — up to five times the regulatory requirement — NATURCLAD-W and NATURCLAD-B retain their appearance and contrast within standard tolerances. The surface composition protects the wood from extreme weather, eliminating any need for oiling, sealing, or refinishing over the life of the façade.

The line spans the whole envelope. NATURCLAD cladding panels sit on ventilated façades; NATURSIDING delivers the same timber as pre-machined boards with a fast closed-joint clip system; NATURSOFFIT handles covered horizontal surfaces and carries the same finishes indoors for true visual continuity; and the GRCLAD fireproof range swaps the cellulose core for a glass-reinforced one to reach non-combustible classifications for high-rise work.

ETG supports Parklex Prodema across Canada from finish selection through installation detailing. Panels can be supplied with the PEFC certificate and an Environmental Product Declaration (EPD), with documentation available for LEED credits — making the range a strong fit for sustainable civic, institutional, residential, and commercial projects, with a 10-year warranty on every product.

// PARKLEX PRODEMA CANADA · THE RANGE

Four systems, one natural-timber language.

From ventilated façade cladding to fast-fit siding, covered soffits, and non-combustible fireproof panels — each Parklex Prodema system shares the same genuine-wood surface and maintenance-free engineered core, so a single material palette can run across an entire building.

// 01 — NATURCLAD
Cladding Panels

Natural wood and super-textured panels for ventilated façades. NATURCLAD-W carries a 0.6 mm natural-wood face; NATURCLAD-B uses a natural-wood veneer. Highly resistant to sun, pollution, and weather.

// VENTILATED FAÇADE
// 02 — NATURSIDING
Timber Siding

The same timber cut into pre-machined boards of varying widths, with a closed-joint clip system for fast, concealed installation — horizontal or vertical, with no visible screws or rivets.

// CLOSED-JOINT CLIP
// 03 — NATURSOFFIT
Soffit Panels

Natural-wood panels for covered horizontal features — ventilated false ceilings and terrace soffits. The same finishes are available indoors, creating seamless visual continuity from outside in.

// INDOOR-OUTDOOR
// 04 — GRCLAD
Fireproof Panels

A fibreglass core impregnated with mineral-filled resins replaces the cellulose layer, producing a non-combustible panel that meets strict high-rise fire regulations — up to A2-s1-d0, tested to BS 8414.

// NON-COMBUSTIBLE
