// CASE STUDY — CANADIAN COLD CLIMATE

Freeze-Thaw Immunity, Verified

Canadian winters drive a cycle of moisture accumulation, freezing, and thawing that destroys conventional roofing membranes within 15-20 years. elZinc's impermeable titanium zinc surface remains unfazed by these conditions.

Even after particularly snowy winters, a temporary white discolouration may appear as snow melts and reveals retained meltwater. This effect gradually fades back to the natural colour of zinc — and is absent entirely on elZinc Rainbow and elZinc Advance finishes.
