// elZINC CANADA · OVERVIEW

elZinc Canada
by Exterior Technologies Group.

Exterior Technologies Group is the Canadian distributor for elZinc — premium architectural rolled zinc manufactured in Spain by Asturiana de Laminados, and supplied to designers in more than 50 countries. Rolled from 99.99%-pure titanium zinc, every coil leaves the mill ready for façade or roofing.

Zinc is the only architectural metal that repairs itself. Exposed to the atmosphere, it forms a self-passivating layer of zinc carbonate — a continuous protective skin that heals scratches and isolates the underlying material from corrosion. The result is a façade that requires effectively no maintenance for more than a century, while developing the soft, mineral tonality that makes zinc one of the most recognizable materials in contemporary architecture.

elZinc takes that material logic further. Its rolling process holds the alloy to 99.99% purity with controlled additions of titanium and copper, producing a metal that forms cleanly, takes a precise seam, and ages predictably across climates. The elZinc Alkimi pre-weathered greys arrive on site already at their finished tone — no waiting years for the patina to develop, and no risk of inconsistent weathering across elevations.

For the design team, the elZinc range removes a long list of unknowns. The Alkimi range carries a lifetime warranty — unique in the metal cladding industry — covering elZinc Natural, Slate, Graphite, Oliva, Crystal, and Lava. The Rainbow range adds six pre-coloured finishes (red, blue, green, black, gold, and brown), each produced by bonding mineral pigments to rolled titanium zinc with a 35μm organic coating for additional corrosion resistance.

ETG distributes the full elZinc system across Canada — coils, pre-formed standing-seam profiles, flat-lock shingles, interlocking panels, and the matching rainwater drainage line. We support design teams from finish selection and detail review through shop drawings and installer coordination, with project examples spanning civic, institutional, multi-residential, and custom residential work across Ontario, Quebec, Alberta, and BC.

// elZINC CANADA · FINISH RANGE

Eleven finishes, two ranges, one lifetime promise.

The elZinc Alkimi range covers the full spectrum of pre-weathered greys — from raw mill-bright Natural through the deep charcoal of Lava — each guaranteed for life. The Rainbow range adds six pre-coloured shades produced by bonding mineral pigments to rolled titanium zinc.

// 01 — ALKIMI
Natural

The original titanium zinc, fresh from the rolling mill. Bright mineral grey that develops a natural patina over time when fully exposed.

// MILL BRIGHT
// 02 — ALKIMI
Slate®

Pre-patinated matte grey — visually identical to naturally weathered zinc, delivered already at its finished tone. The most widely specified Alkimi finish.

// MATTE GREY
// 03 — ALKIMI
Graphite®

A custom near-black architectural zinc. Pure, sober, and high-contrast — equally capable of blending into a dark envelope or standing out against light masonry.

// NEAR BLACK
// 04 — ALKIMI
Oliva®

Subtle warm olive-grey. Softens contemporary geometries and works particularly well alongside timber, stone, and natural-tone masonry.

// WARM GREY
// 05 — ALKIMI
Crystal®

Pearl grey pre-patinated zinc with shifting reflections that change with light angle and weather. The most luminous finish in the Alkimi range.

// PEARL GREY
// 06 — ALKIMI
Lava®

Charcoal grey pre-weathered zinc — deeper than Slate, lighter than Graphite. Anchors a greyscale palette across complex façade compositions.

// CHARCOAL
// 07 — RAINBOW
Rainbow® Copper

Warm copper-brown pre-coloured zinc. Mineral pigment bonded to rolled titanium zinc with a 35μm organic coating for additional corrosion protection.

// PRE-COLOURED
// 08 — RAINBOW
Rainbow® Range

Five additional pre-coloured shades — Red, Gold, Green, Blue, Brown, and Black. Custom colours available on request for signature projects.
