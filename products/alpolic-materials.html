Alpolic combines two thin sheets of aluminium with a non-aluminium core, creating a material that is rigid yet lightweight, easy to fabricate, and capable of holding any architectural form imaginable.

// CASE STUDY — MOODY CENTER

50,000 sq ft of Origami Geometry

The Moody Center in Austin, Texas required a façade that could perform under intense solar exposure while expressing distinct visual identity. ALPOLIC/fr panels — appearing square but actually trapezoid in nature — wrap the structure in 50,000 sq ft of complex geometry.

Working with KSC, Inc. as fabricator, the project achieved LEED Gold certification while creating a design that places the venue among the most distinctive arenas in North America.
