Enhance Your Building with ForMe Design Soffits

ForMe Design soffits offer a blend of style and functionality that elevates your exterior beyond the façade. The SKIN line wraps from wall to soffit in a continuous aluminium plane.

// FIG. 01 — FORME DESIGN SKIN SOFFIT / WALNUT POWDER

ForMe Design soffits offer a blend of style and functionality that elevates your exterior beyond the façade. As part of the SKIN architectural cladding line from Italy's DVF Group, the soffit profile delivers continuous aesthetic from vertical wall surface to horizontal overhang — eliminating the visual break that typically separates these surfaces.

Why It Matters

Soffits are an often-overlooked element of building exteriors. Yet they're visually prominent — particularly at entrances, drop-offs, and covered walkways where occupants experience them at close range. ForMe SKIN soffits transform this overlooked surface into a deliberate design feature.

"Beyond the façade — eliminating the conventional break between vertical and horizontal surfaces."

— ForMe Design

The SKIN System

The SKIN system uses 100% extruded aluminium planks ranging from 30mm to 200mm in width, with concealed-fastener installation that delivers a continuous face without visible hardware. The same plank can wrap from vertical wall application onto a horizontal soffit, maintaining grain alignment and module continuity.

Powder on Powder

The standout finish for soffit applications is ForMe's Powder on Powder woodgrain. Two layers of powder coating produce authentic woodgrain depth and texture — the visual character of walnut, oak, or teak, with the durability, fire performance, and decade-long colour stability of architectural aluminium.

Performance Highlights

  • 15-year warranty — industry-leading for architectural aluminium soffits
  • A2-s1, d0 fire rating — non-combustible
  • 100% recyclable — closed-loop aluminium
  • 50+ years of DVF Group expertise — Italian manufacturing heritage
