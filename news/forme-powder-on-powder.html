ForMe Design: Powder on Powder Innovation

The Powder on Powder technology brings authentic woodgrain finishes to extruded aluminium cladding, with industry-leading 15-year warranty coverage on the resulting finish.

// FIG. 01 — POWDER ON POWDER WOODGRAIN / 15-YEAR WARRANTY

The Powder on Powder finishing technology represents a significant step forward for architectural aluminium cladding. By applying two layers of powder coating in sequence — a base coat establishing the wood undertone, followed by a textured grain pattern — ForMe produces a woodgrain finish indistinguishable from natural wood at typical viewing distance.

The Process

Conventional woodgrain finishes on aluminium use either sublimation (heat transfer of pattern from a film) or HD printing (direct ink-jet application). Both produce visually convincing results but lack the surface texture of real wood.

Powder on Powder fundamentally differs by building the grain pattern in physical relief on the aluminium surface. The base powder layer establishes colour. The grain layer is then applied through a patented process that creates micro-relief texture aligned with the visual grain. The result is a finish you can feel — not just see.

"The visual character of walnut, oak, or teak — with the durability of architectural aluminium and 15-year finish warranty."

— ForMe Design

Performance Standards

The Powder on Powder finish carries a 15-year warranty — significantly longer than typical PVDF or polyester powder coatings on aluminium. Performance verification includes:

  • Salt spray testing — 1,000 hours per ASTM B117
  • UV exposure — QUV accelerated weathering per ASTM G154
  • Humidity testing — per ASTM D2247
  • Fire performance — A2-s1, d0 (non-combustible per EN 13501-1)

Available Colours

The standard Powder on Powder palette includes Walnut, Oak, Teak, Cedar, Mahogany, and Wenge — each available in matte and satin sheen. Custom colours are available with project-specific minimum order quantities.
