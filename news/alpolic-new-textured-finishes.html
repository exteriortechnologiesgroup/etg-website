First Textured FEVE Paint System Available for Metal Composite Materials

(CHESAPEAKE, Va.) April 1, 2025 — Mitsubishi Chemical America's (MCA) ALPOLIC Division, a leader in metal composite material (MCM) manufacturing, is pleased to announce the launch of its new metal composite material finishes featuring the first FEVE textured paint system.

"It's unique because you can see and feel it," said Octavio Diaz, ALPOLIC sales director. "It's multidimensional and surprisingly reflective because of the way the surface texture responds to light." The finish was created in collaboration with our world-renowned coatings supplier, Sherwin-Williams® Coil Coatings, as an additional option for architects and designers wanting the look of a textured product but with the inherent characteristics of ALPOLIC materials.

ALPOLIC Textured MCM

"We've received numerous requests for a textured finish and are pleased to now offer this to our customers," said Diaz. "The best part is that it can be used with nearly any FEVE color imaginable," continued Diaz. "This paint system offers bright color and product longevity — minimizing color fading, chalking or peeling."

Like all ALPOLIC products, the Textured Series comes with the industry's leading Repair and Replace warranty, along with the durability and exceptional adaptability for which it is known. Learn more about this exciting finish here.

ALPOLIC's Textured MCM Series

Before, you had to choose between the durability and strength of MCM or the look and feel of a textured product.

We believe there's a better way. You shouldn't have to compromise when it comes to your vision. You asked for it, and we delivered: the durability and safety of MCM, with a beautiful textured surface.

ALPOLIC's Textured Series provides all the benefits of our MCM with the added interest and dimension of texture. You deserve a product worthy of your iconic projects.
