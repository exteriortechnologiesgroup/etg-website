ALPOLIC Timber Series: New Japanese Birch & Wild Plum

Discover the new Japanese Birch and Wild Plum finishes in the ALPOLIC Timber Series — wood-inspired aesthetics with the high performance of metal composite materials.

// FIG. 01 — ALPOLIC TIMBER SERIES / NEW PATTERNS

ALPOLIC has expanded its Timber Series with two new wood-inspired finishes — Japanese Birch and Wild Plum — both available now through Exterior Technologies Group across Canada.

The Timber Series uses an advanced image transfer process to produce authentic wood-grain finishes on aluminium composite materials. The result: the natural look of wood without the weight, maintenance burden, or expense.

Japanese Birch

The Japanese Birch finish reproduces the pale, finely-grained character of premium birch lumber. Designed for projects seeking a Scandinavian or Japandi aesthetic, the finish reads as natural wood at distance and pairs effortlessly with charcoal mullions and warm metal accents.

Wild Plum

Wild Plum is the warmer of the two new finishes — a deep mahogany tone with subtle reddish grain. The pattern works particularly well on signature retail, hospitality, and high-end residential applications where warm character is essential.

"Achieve wood-inspired aesthetics with high-performance metal composite materials for your next building envelope."

— ALPOLIC Product Brief

Why Timber Series

Wood cladding remains a desirable aesthetic but presents persistent maintenance, weather, and fire-performance challenges in commercial applications. The Timber Series resolves these trade-offs:

  • Fire performance: ALPOLIC/fr core meets CAN/ULC S134
  • Weather: 20-year PVDF warranty against finish degradation
  • Weight: Aluminium composite at a fraction of solid wood weight
  • Consistency: No grain irregularities, no seasonal movement
  • Maintenance: Wash-only, no staining or refinishing required
