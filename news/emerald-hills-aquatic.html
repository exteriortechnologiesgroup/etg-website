Pan Am Aquatic Centre: TAC Spacer at Scale

The Pan Am Aquatic facility leverages TAC Thermal Spacer to achieve 97% reduction in thermal bridging across its rainscreen wall system — paired with EPIC Metals roof deck above.

// FIG. 01 — PAN AM AQUATICS / TAC SPACER + EPIC DECK

The Pan Am Aquatic Centre in Toronto required envelope and roof systems engineered to performance standards well above conventional aquatic facility benchmarks. Two ETG-represented products played central roles: TAC Thermal Spacer for the rainscreen sub-frame, and EPIC Metals Wideck for the exposed roof system.

Rainscreen Performance

TAC Thermal Spacer 2.0 was specified across the full façade. The fiberglass-composite clip eliminated the thermal bridge while delivering full code wind uplift capacity — verified through third-party hot box testing. Effective wall R-value tested to R-27.7, significantly above code minimum and a meaningful contribution to facility energy performance.

"Epic Metals Roof Deck Ceiling System lining the roofing in a clean modern finish — and ALPOLIC/fr at its finest framing the stairway."

— ETG Project Brief

EPIC Metals Wideck

Above the pool deck, EPIC Metals Wideck delivers two functions in one assembly: structural roof deck capable of clear-spanning the pool volume, and architectural ceiling finish requiring no secondary suspended ceiling. The exposed deck profile is engineered for acoustic performance — perforated soffits achieving NRC 0.85+.

Cross-Product Coordination

The same project pairs EPIC Metals deck above with ALPOLIC/fr panels at the central stairway — demonstrating cross-product coordination across the ETG portfolio. This kind of integrated specification, where multiple ETG-represented systems work together within a single project, is increasingly common as design teams recognize the value of coordinated technical support.

Project Highlights

  • TAC 2.0 Thermal Spacer — full façade rainscreen
  • 97% thermal bridging reduction
  • R-27.7 effective wall R-value
  • EPIC Metals Wideck — exposed roof / ceiling
  • NRC 0.85+ acoustic performance
  • ALPOLIC/fr — stairway feature wall
