ALPOLIC-fr: Shaping Architectural Marvels at the Moody Center

ALPOLIC/fr metal composite materials, integrated by KSC, Inc., define the Moody Center's aesthetic identity. The 50,000 sq ft of aluminum panels feature complex origami geometry that contributes to LEED Gold certification.

// FIG. 01 — MOODY CENTER FAÇADE / 50,000 SQ FT ALPOLIC/fr

The Moody Center in Austin, Texas required a façade that could perform under the city's intense solar exposure while expressing distinct visual identity for one of the region's most ambitious arena projects in decades. The team at KSC, Inc. — fabricator and installer for the project — turned to ALPOLIC/fr aluminium composite materials to deliver both performance and presence.

While the ALPOLIC/fr panels appear square in photos, they are actually origami / trapezoid in nature, not perfectly flat. The 50,000 sq ft of aluminum panels and their complex geometry add a unique design feature that places the Moody Center among the most distinctive arenas in North America.

Why ALPOLIC/fr

The choice of ALPOLIC/fr was driven by three interlocking project requirements: fire performance, formability, and aesthetic continuity. The fire-resistant mineral core of /fr meets the most stringent CAN/ULC S134 and ASTM E84 requirements — non-negotiable for a venue of this scale.

Beyond beauty, ALPOLIC materials strategically manage solar exposure. The panel geometry was designed to shade interior surfaces during peak solar hours, contributing meaningfully to the project's LEED Gold certification target.

"The complex geometry adds a unique design feature that places the Moody Center among incredible structures."

— ETG Project Brief

Beyond the Façade

The ALPOLIC roots actually trace to the petroleum industry. For over four decades, the materials have appeared on fuel canopies and station convenience stores throughout North America. That heritage of weather and chemical exposure resistance translates directly to high-performance architectural cladding.

For more than 40 years, ALPOLIC has offered an extensive selection of rich, vibrant colours and styles for both painted and natural metal surfaces — bringing design intent into reality across landmark projects worldwide.

Panel Specifications Used

The Moody Center project specified ALPOLIC/fr 4mm aluminium composite material with a custom finish from the AAMA 2605 PVDF range. Panels were CNC-cut, routed, and folded into the trapezoid origami geometry by KSC, Inc., then field-installed onto a pre-engineered sub-frame.
