// ALPOLIC /FR — FIRE-RATED METAL COMPOSITE

Setting the Stage: The Moody Center's Grand Debut

In the heart of Austin, the Moody Center emerged as a beacon of entertainment in April 2022. A $375 million project led by Oak View Group, designed by Gensler's architects, this 15,000-seat arena became the new vibrant home for University of Texas Longhorns basketball and a versatile stage for an array of live performances.

Designing for Harmony: A Fusion of Purpose and Community

University-oriented spaces like the Moody Center bridge academia and community. Designed for music with a nod to sporting events, this 530,000 square-foot arena emphasizes fellowship among attendees while harmonizing seamlessly with the campus's architectural ethos.

Crafting Excellence: The Role of Materials and Precision Engineering

ALPOLIC/fr metal composite materials, meticulously integrated by KSC, Inc., define the Moody Center's aesthetic. Beyond beauty, these materials strategically manage solar exposure, contributing to LEED Gold certification. The engineering finesse in mounting fin sunshades behind MCM panels showcases KSC's expertise and precision, meeting tight deadlines before the arena's inaugural concerts.

A Testament to Success: The Moody Center's Impact

Beyond its colossal stature, the Moody Center radiates warmth and intimacy. Local art, exclusive lounges, and an airy design foster an inviting atmosphere. Its customizable spaces cater to diverse needs, serving as a University of Texas cornerstone and a dynamic community hub attracting global events and entertainment.

Praising Architectural Brilliance: Honors and Accolades

Renowned for its resilience and aesthetic allure, the Moody Center integrates flawlessly into the hillside terrain, offering efficient crowd movement. Luxurious amenities and breathtaking city views from the terrace underscore its architectural prowess, earning prestigious awards and accolades since its opening.

Material Excellence in Entertainment Design: ALPOLIC/fr MCM

ALPOLIC/fr metal composite materials stand as the pinnacle choice for high-profile entertainment venues like the Moody Center. Balancing durability, safety, and aesthetics, ALPOLIC's fire-resistant properties and diverse color palette make it an ideal cladding choice for grand, bustling spaces.

Partnering for Quality: ALPOLIC through Exterior Technologies Group in Canada

In Canada, Exterior Technologies Group stands as the authorized reseller of ALPOLIC materials, providing access to ALPOLIC/fr MCM for innovative architectural projects. Their commitment to quality and ALPOLIC's industry-leading warranty ensure that Canadian architectural designs benefit from ALPOLIC's exceptional attributes, meeting stringent safety standards while offering a wide array of design possibilities.
