elZinc Rainbow: Six Coloured Variations

The elZinc Rainbow range delivers natural, warm, attractive graded colours — red, blue, green, black, gold, and brown — for distinctive architectural projects.

// FIG. 01 — ELZINC RAINBOW / SIX COLOURED VARIANTS

elZinc Rainbow is available in a range of natural, warm, and attractive graded colours: red, blue, green, black, gold, and brown. Each variant retains the underlying performance characteristics of titanium zinc — including freeze-thaw immunity and decade-spanning service life — while delivering a refined coloured aesthetic that conventional zinc cannot achieve.

The Six Colours

The standard Rainbow palette includes:

  • Rainbow Red — deep oxblood with subtle copper undertones
  • Rainbow Blue — slate blue inspired by patinated copper
  • Rainbow Green — deep verdigris with warm depth
  • Rainbow Black — refined matte black with zinc base
  • Rainbow Gold — warm bronze tones
  • Rainbow Brown — earthy mahogany undertone

"Enjoy a great deal of freedom when designing with our wide range of finishes — natural, warm, and attractive graded colours."

— elZinc

Key Differentiator

Unlike powder-coated or PVDF-finished aluminium, elZinc Rainbow colour is integrated into the zinc surface through a proprietary process — not applied as a coating. The result: no chip, no peel, no flake. Even deep scratches reveal more of the same coloured zinc beneath.

Rainbow finishes are also immune to white discolouration after snow melt — a meaningful differentiator for Canadian projects facing aggressive winter conditions.

Architectural Applications

Rainbow finishes are particularly compelling for hospitality, civic, cultural, and high-end residential projects where distinctive material identity is part of the design intent. The colour palette pairs effectively with both warm wood elements and contemporary glass & concrete.

Sample swatches and project-specific consultation are available now through ETG's technical team.
