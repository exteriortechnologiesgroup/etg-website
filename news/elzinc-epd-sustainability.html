Sustainability & Innovation: elZinc's EPDs

How Exterior Technologies Group and elZinc are leading the way in sustainable cladding solutions through Environmental Product Declarations and ISO 14067 carbon footprint certification.

// FIG. 01 — ELZINC EPD CERTIFICATION / ISO 14067

At Exterior Technologies Group, we are dedicated to providing high-performance exterior cladding solutions that emphasize durability, sustainability, and design flexibility. As part of our commitment to bringing innovative façade systems to the Canadian market, we partner with industry-leading manufacturers like elZinc — ensuring that architects and builders have access to materials that meet today's architectural demands.

Why EPDs Matter

Sustainability is at the core of modern architecture, and Environmental Product Declarations (EPDs) play a crucial role in assessing the environmental impact of building materials. An EPD is an independently verified document that provides transparent, comparable data on the life-cycle environmental impact of a product.

EPDs are widely recognized in green building certification programs such as LEED and BREEAM, making them essential tools for sustainable construction. For Canadian projects targeting LEED v4 Materials & Resources credits, EPD-certified products are increasingly required rather than optional.

elZinc's Carbon Certification

elZinc has secured the carbon footprint certification in accordance with the UNE-EN ISO14067:2019 standard. This certification applies to the elZinc Alkimi (natural zinc and five grey pre-patinated variations) and elZinc Rainbow product ranges.

"This achievement is a testament to elZinc's unwavering dedication to upholding industry standards that contribute to the global fight against climate change."

— elZinc

What's Measured

The Carbon Footprint certification comprehensively measures total greenhouse gas (GHG) emissions throughout the entire lifecycle of the product:

  • Extraction and Production of Raw Materials — initial acquisition and processing of zinc
  • Transport of Raw Materials — environmental impact of bringing raw materials to elZinc's plant
  • Manufacture of elZinc Coil and Sheet — production of Natural, Alkimi, and Rainbow lines

For ETG, this certification underscores elZinc's commitment to quality and sustainability while reinforcing the company's alignment with Canada's growing emphasis on sustainable construction materials and responsible environmental stewardship.
