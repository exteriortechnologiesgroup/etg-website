Bring a manufacturer-grade technical session to your studio. Each ETG Lunch & Learn is a focused 45-minute presentation built for architects, designers, and specification writers — covering the material, the detailing, and the specification of a complete building-envelope system.

Technical sessions,
delivered to your team.

We bring each presentation to your office — or deliver it virtually — at a time that suits your schedule. Sessions run 45 minutes with a further 15 minutes for project-specific questions.

Several presentations are AIA-approved for continuing professional education credits, with OAA CORE credits available.
