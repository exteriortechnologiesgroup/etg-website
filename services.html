What We Do

Innovative
Design
Solutions

We provide bespoke design services that creatively address the specific needs of each project — high-quality, visually compelling exteriors that perform over time across the full range of Canadian conditions.
SVC-01

Design Consultation

Tailored proposals, in-depth material selection, integration with architectural style, visual and functional harmony, and compliance with local building codes. We work with architects from concept through detail design.

SVC-02

Design Assist

Collaboration, customization, intelligent insights and recommendations, budget optimization, and architectural detailing at every stage. Our team participates throughout pre-construction to keep your design intent intact.

SVC-03

Material Specification

Detailed material analysis, durability considerations, cost-effective solutions, aesthetic compatibility, and environmental impact assessment. We help spec writers translate design intent into accurate, performance-driven product calls.

SVC-04

AutoCAD Details

Roof and wall details delivered in editable AutoCAD format. Standard, custom, and project-specific drawings — complete with code references, fastener callouts, and sequencing notes ready for your CD set.

SVC-05

Budget Pricing

High-level budget pricing for early-stage scoping. Our pricing accounts for product cost, sub-frame, accessories, and typical installation conditions — giving design teams accurate cost intelligence at concept stage.

How We Work

A Five-Phase
Engagement

// PHASE 01

Discovery

Initial consultation to understand project scope, design intent, performance requirements, and budget envelope.

// PHASE 02

Selection

Material recommendations, sample delivery, finish selection, and confirmation of compatibility with adjacent assemblies.

// PHASE 03

Specification

Spec writing assistance, AutoCAD details, BIM coordination support, and compliance documentation.

// PHASE 04

Pricing

High-level budget pricing during DD, refined to firm pricing at CDs. Value engineering options provided when required.

// PHASE 05

Field Review

On-site field review during installation, mock-up review, and post-installation verification of performance criteria.
