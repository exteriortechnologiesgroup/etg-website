// THE PRESENTATION

A comprehensive technical presentation on metal composite material (MCM/ACM) wall systems, built around the full ALPOLIC Materials product line. The session is designed for architects, designers, and specifiers working with exterior metal composite wall panel systems, and covers the material from manufacture through fabrication, finishes, and wall-system detailing.

Mitsubishi is a global leader in the development and manufacture of metal composite materials for exterior and interior construction. ALPOLIC is an approved and certified aluminum composite material for use throughout North America — its industry-leading warranties, breadth of finishes, and fire-resistant core have made it one of the most specified ACM products available.

Finishes range from solid colours to wood-grain and natural-stone looks, and ALPOLIC can be produced in copper, titanium, zinc, and stainless steel (brushed and mirror). Twenty-year paint warranties are standard, and production is ISO certified.
